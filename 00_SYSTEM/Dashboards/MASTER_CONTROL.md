## Decision filter

Before adding any feature, ask:

1. Does it reduce accidental gluten exposure?
2. Does it increase trust?
3. Can it be maintained reliably?
4. Is it understandable to users?
5. Does it strengthen the moat?

If no to most answers → do not prioritise.
