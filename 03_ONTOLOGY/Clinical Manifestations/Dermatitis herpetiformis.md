# Dermatitis herpetiformis

Dermatitis herpetiformis is a chronic skin condition associated with reactions to [[Gluten]].

It is considered a cutaneous manifestation of [[Coeliac disease]] and involves autoimmune processes.

## Immune mechanism
- [[IgA immune response]]

## Skin manifestations
- [[Skin inflammation]]
- [[Itching]]
- [[Blistering rash]]

## Associated conditions
- [[Coeliac disease]]

## Trigger sources
- [[Wheat]]
- [[Barley]]
- [[Rye]]

## Management
- [[Gluten-free diet]]
Part of: [[Gluten-related disorders]]
