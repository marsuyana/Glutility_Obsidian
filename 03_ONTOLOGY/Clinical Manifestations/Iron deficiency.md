
Iron deficiency is a common nutritional consequence of [[Malabsorption]] in conditions such as [[Coeliac disease]].

Damage to the [[Small intestine]] reduces the body's ability to absorb dietary iron.

## Possible symptoms
- [[Fatigue]]
- [[Weakness]]
- [[Brain fog]]
- [[Pale skin]]
- [[Shortness of breath]]

## Underlying mechanisms
- [[Malabsorption]]
- [[Villous atrophy]]

## Associated condition
- [[Coeliac disease]]
