Vitamin B12 deficiency

Vitamin B12 deficiency may occur in [[Coeliac disease]] due to [[Malabsorption]] in the damaged [[Small intestine]].

## Possible symptoms
- [[Fatigue]]
- [[Brain fog]]
- [[Numbness]]
- [[Tingling]]

## Underlying mechanisms
- [[Malabsorption]]

## Associated condition
- [[Coeliac disease]]
