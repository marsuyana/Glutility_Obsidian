
Gluten ataxia is a neurological disorder associated with immune reactions to [[Gluten]].

It affects coordination and balance due to damage to parts of the nervous system.

## Immune mechanism
- [[IgA immune response]]

## Neurological mechanisms
- [[Gut–brain axis]]
- [[Immune activation]]
- [[Peripheral neuropathy]]

## Symptoms
- [[Peripheral neuropathy]]
- [[Balance problems]]
- [[Coordination difficulties]]

## Associated conditions
- [[Coeliac disease]]

## Trigger sources
- [[Wheat]]
- [[Barley]]
- [[Rye]]

## Management
- [[Gluten-free diet]]
