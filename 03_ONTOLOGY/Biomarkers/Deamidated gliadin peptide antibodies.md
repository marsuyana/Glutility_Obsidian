[[Deamidated gliadin peptide antibodies]]

Deamidated gliadin peptide (DGP) antibodies are biomarkers used in evaluating [[Coeliac disease]] and other [[Gluten]]-related disorders.

They may appear as part of both the [[IgA immune response]] and [[IgG immune response]].

## Associated conditions
- [[Coeliac disease]]
- [[Non-coeliac gluten sensitivity]]

## Related biomarkers
- [[tTG antibodies]]
- [[EMA antibodies]]

## Diagnostic role
- [[Coeliac disease screening]]
