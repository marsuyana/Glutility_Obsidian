# EMA antibodies

Endomysial antibodies (EMA) are highly specific antibodies used in confirming the diagnosis of [[Coeliac disease]].

They are part of the [[IgA immune response]] triggered by exposure to [[Gluten]].

## Associated condition
- [[Coeliac disease]]

## Related immune pathway
- [[IgA immune response]]

## Related biomarkers
- [[tTG antibodies]]
- [[Deamidated gliadin peptide antibodies]]

## Diagnostic role
- [[Coeliac disease diagnosis]]
