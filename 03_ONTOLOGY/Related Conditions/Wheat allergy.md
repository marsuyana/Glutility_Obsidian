# Wheat allergy

Wheat allergy is an allergic reaction to proteins found in [[Wheat]]. It is mediated by an [[IgE immune response]] and is different from [[Coeliac disease]].

## Immune mechanism
- [[IgE immune response]]

## Trigger sources
- [[Wheat]]
- [[Wheat proteins]]

## Common symptoms
- [[Hives]]
- [[Swelling]]
- [[Respiratory reactions]]
- [[Anaphylaxis]]

## Related immune reactions
- [[Allergic reaction]]
- [[Mast cell activation]]

## Management
- [[Avoidance of wheat]]
- 
Part of: [[Gluten-related disorders]]
