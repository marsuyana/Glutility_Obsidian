# Non-coeliac gluten sensitivity

Non-coeliac gluten sensitivity (NCGS) describes symptoms that occur after ingestion of [[Gluten]] in individuals who do not have [[Coeliac disease]] or [[Wheat allergy]].

## Possible immune pathways
- [[IgG immune response]]
- [[Innate immune activation]]

## Possible triggers
- [[Gluten peptides]]
- [[Amylase-trypsin inhibitors]]
- [[FODMAP carbohydrates]]

## Related conditions
- [[Irritable bowel syndrome]]
## Symptoms
- [[Bloating]]
- [[Abdominal pain]]
- [[Fatigue]]
- [[Brain fog]]
- [[Headache]]

## Management
- [[Gluten-free diet]]

Part of: [[Gluten-related disorders]]
