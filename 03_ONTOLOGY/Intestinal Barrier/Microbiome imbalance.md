Microbiome imbalance

Microbiome imbalance (also called dysbiosis) refers to disruption of the normal microbial community in the gut.

Changes in the gut microbiome may influence immune responses and intestinal barrier function in people reacting to [[Gluten]].

## Related mechanisms
- [[Increased intestinal permeability]]
- [[Immune activation]]
- [[Intestinal Inflammation]]

## Associated conditions
- [[Coeliac disease]]
- [[Non-coeliac gluten sensitivity]]
