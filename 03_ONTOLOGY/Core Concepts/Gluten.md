
Gluten is a protein complex found in certain cereal grains.  
It is primarily composed of [[Glutenin]] and [[Gliadin]]] and is responsible for the elasticity of dough.
## Gluten components

- [[Gliadin]]
- [[Glutenin]]

## Source grains

- [[Wheat]]
- [[Barley]]
- [[Rye]]
- [[Triticale]]
- [[Oats]]
