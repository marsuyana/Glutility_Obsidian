
Gluten-related disorders are conditions triggered by immune or physiological reactions to gluten or gluten-containing grains.

## Autoimmune

- [[Coeliac disease]]
- [[Dermatitis herpetiformis]]
- [[Gluten ataxia]]

These conditions involve autoimmune responses triggered by gluten ingestion.

## Allergic

- [[Wheat allergy]]

This condition is mediated by an IgE immune response to proteins in wheat.

## Non-autoimmune sensitivity

- [[Non-coeliac gluten sensitivity]]

Symptoms occur after gluten ingestion but without the autoimmune markers of coeliac disease or IgE-mediated allergy.

## Shared triggers

Common trigger grains include:

- [[Wheat]]
- [[Barley]]
- [[Rye]]
- [[Triticale]]

## Common management

- [[Gluten-free diet]]
