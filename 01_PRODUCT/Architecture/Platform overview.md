
The Gluten Exposure Scanner platform is designed to help individuals with [[Coeliac disease]] reduce exposure to [[Gluten]] by analysing food products, restaurant environments, and personal exposure history.

The system combines ingredient analysis, contamination modelling, and user-reported data to evaluate potential gluten exposure risks.

This page provides a high-level overview of the platform architecture.

- [[Cross-contamination model]]

# Purpose of the platform

The Gluten Exposure Scanner platform aims to:

- improve daily safety for individuals with [[Coeliac disease]]
    
- reduce accidental exposure to [[Gluten]]
    
- improve transparency in food systems
    
- support research on gluten exposure patterns
    

The platform integrates ingredient knowledge, contamination risk modelling, and real-world food safety data.

Together these systems form the **Gluten Exposure Scanner platform used by Glutility**.
