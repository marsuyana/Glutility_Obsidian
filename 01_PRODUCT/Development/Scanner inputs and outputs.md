
## Purpose
Define exactly what information the scanner receives and what the user receives in return.

---

## Core inputs

### Product scan
- Barcode
- Product name
- Brand
- Ingredients list
- Allergen statement
- Certification labels

### Restaurant / venue
- Venue name
- Cuisine type
- Menu item
- User notes
- Staff response
- Shared fryer / kitchen indicators

### User context
- Coeliac disease
- Gluten sensitivity
- Risk tolerance setting
- Previous reactions
- Saved preferences

### Future inputs
- Photo scan
- OCR label reading
- Receipt import
- Community reports

---

## Core outputs

### Risk result
- SAFE
- CAUTION
- HIGH RISK
- UNKNOWN

### Confidence level
- High
- Medium
- Low

### Explanation
- Ingredient risk detected
- Cross-contamination concern
- Missing data
- Certified gluten-free evidence

### Recommended action
- Safe to consider
- Verify with venue
- Avoid
- Need more information

### Memory / tracking
- Save scan result
- Add notes
- Log symptoms
- View history

---

## Product rules
- Show reasoning clearly
- Never overclaim certainty
- Prefer trust over convenience
- Unknown is valid when evidence is weak
