# Language standards

All documentation in the vault should follow **British English spelling**.

Example:

- coeliac disease
    
- fibre
    
- flavour
    
- organisation
    
- analyse
    

However, the American spelling **celiac** should remain searchable through page aliases.

# Platform objective

The scanner platform is designed to help individuals with [[Coeliac disease]] avoid exposure to [[Gluten]] by evaluating:

- packaged food products
    
- restaurant environments
    
- contamination risks
    
- personal exposure history
    

The system integrates:

- [[Ingredient risk engine]]
    
- [[Cross-contamination model]]
    
- [[Restaurant safety database]]
    
- [[Product database]]
    
- [[Exposure tracking]]
    

Together these components form the **Gluten Exposure Scanner platform** used by Glutility.
